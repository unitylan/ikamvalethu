About us

We exist to unlock potential through skills and opportunity.

Ikamva Lethu Skills Center is a community development initiative established to empower rural communities through internationally recognized, workplace-accredited skills training. Our focus is to bridge the gap between rural youth and global employment opportunities by offering high-quality, industry-aligned technology and digital skills programs.  Founded as a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), Ikamva Lethu is built on the belief that every young person, regardless of background or location, deserves access to world-class education, certification, and pathways to meaningful employment.

Based in Butter Worth Est. 2024 Community-first

Our Vision

To become a leading rural-based skills and innovation hub that produces globally competitive, work-ready youth equipped with internationally accredited qualifications.

Our Mission

  • To deliver accredited, hands-on training programs that meet international industry standards.
  • To partner with leading global technology companies to bring world-class learning opportunities to rural communities.
  • To create sustainable employment pathways for rural youth.
  • To promote digital inclusion and reduce poverty through skills development.

Our Purpose

Rural communities often face systemic barriers to accessing quality, globally recognized training. Ikamva Lethu Skills Center exists to remove those barriers by providing:

Accredited Training

Industry-recognised IT and digital skills programs aligned with global standards.

Career Readiness

Practical workplace preparation, mentorship, and employability coaching.

Job Placement Support

Connections to employers and pathways into meaningful employment.

Entrepreneurship

Support for youth-led businesses and innovation-driven initiatives.
